Jai Ganesh Jai Ganesh Aarti "Jai Ganesh Jai Ganesh Jai Ganesh Deva, Mata Jaki Parvati Pita Mahadeva" is one of the most beloved and widely sung Aartis in Hindu devotion. Traditionally, Lord Ganesh, the son of Lord Shiva, is worshipped before all other deities. He is revered as the remover of obstacles and the giver of wisdom, making his worship essential before commencing any Puja or auspicious endeavor.
