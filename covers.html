From India To 'PERTH' | Tamil Version | A Tribute To Bon Iver

The gripping backdrop of Bon Iver's original song "PERTH" left a lasting impression, which was followed by an afterthought of celebrating one's life after their passing. This led to the creation of an Indianized interpretation of "PERTH." This rendition features the rich hues of rustic serenity created by ethnic Indian instruments, set against the backdrop of the world's oldest spoken language, Tamil, and penned by Muthamil. The performance is delivered by celebrated soloists representing diverse musical backgrounds. They collectively represent India in expressing sentiments of loss, hope, and resurrection.
